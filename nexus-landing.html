The problem

The model hub you rely on
is one takedown away from collapse

⚠
Models disappear overnight

A legal notice, a policy change, a geopolitical decision — and years of community work vanishes from a centralized server.

Mistral's Mixtral-8x22B was briefly restricted. Chinese models routinely get flagged. Every link is a liability.
◎
Creators earn exactly $0

You fine-tuned a model that gets downloaded 100,000 times. The platform benefited. You got nothing. Open source with no economic model is charity, not sustainability.

GitHub stars don't pay cloud bills. Neither does HuggingFace clout.
⬡
No chain of custody for data

Which datasets trained this model? Was your content in the crawl? Current infrastructure has no answer. Lawsuits are filling the vacuum.

NYT v. OpenAI made clear: opaque training data is a ticking liability.
